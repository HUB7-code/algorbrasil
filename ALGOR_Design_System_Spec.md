Layout Patterns & Responsive Grid5.1 The "Marketing Chin" PatternFor product cards and landing sections:Image: Occupies top 75-80% of the container. Full bleed.Chin: Bottom 20-25% contains Title, Description, and CTA (Call to Action).Background: "Chin" background is Surface Container, distinct from the image background.5.2 Breakpoints (Adaptive)Implement the following standard breakpoints:Compact: < 600dp (4 Columns, 16dp margins)Medium: 600dp - 839dp (8 Columns, 32dp margins)Expanded: 840dp+ (12 Columns, max-width 1200dp centered)6. Motion & Physics6.1 Animation TokensAvoid linear easing. Use spring physics or standard M3 easing curves.Emphasized (Attention): cubic-bezier(0.2, 0.0, 0.0, 1.0)Usage: Elements entering screen, expanding cards.Standard (Neutral): cubic-bezier(0.4, 0.0, 0.2, 1.0)Usage: Simple state changes, exiting elements.6.2 Duration TokensShort: 100ms (Micro-interactions, hover)Medium: 300ms (Dialogs, Menu expansion)Long: 500ms+ (Page transitions)7.
